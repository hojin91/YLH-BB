about us

Welcome to Bean Boutique Coffeeshop, where we're passionate about serving high-quality coffee and creating a warm and inviting atmosphere for our customers.

Our Vision

At Bean Boutique Coffeeshop, our vision is to become the go-to destination for coffee lovers in Myanmar. We aim to create a unique and memorable experience for every customer, every time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to source the finest coffee beans from around the world and to craft each cup with love and care. We strive to build a community of coffee enthusiasts who share our passion for quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Our History

Bean Boutique Coffeeshop, founded in 2010, began as a small, cozy caf√© in the heart of downtown, dedicated to serving artisanal coffee and handcrafted pastries. Its emphasis on quality and community quickly earned it a loyal following. Over the years, Bean Boutique expanded to multiple locations, becoming a local favorite for its inviting atmosphere, innovative brews, and commitment to sustainable practices. Today, it remains a beloved hub for coffee enthusiasts and a symbol of artisanal excellence in the coffee industry.
